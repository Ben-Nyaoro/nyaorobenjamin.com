Word Guru

Project Summary

The Problem

Learning a new language can be very challenging especially for adults. It is even worse if your are expected to use it in your work or as a language of instruction in your studies. Amongs the many difficulties of learning a new language, is lack of time, lack of opportunities and lack of funds to pay to a language intitution.

The Potential Solution

Word Guru ia a language learning app designed for anyone who would like to learn a new language at their convenience. It allows you to be in charge of your learning schedule, offers you the opportunity to learn a variety of languages and the best part is that it is free for life.

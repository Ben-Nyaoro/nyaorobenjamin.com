SkillMaster

Project Summary

The Problem

Getting onboarded onto a professional software especially if it involves accounting where money and the complex tax issue is concerned can be a great challenge. This is considering that accounting software are developed in one part of the world and used accross the globe. Bearing in mind that every country have some specifics on accounting and taxes and are not uniform accross the globe.

The Potential Solution

Developing an online learning platform where new users of the various accounting systems can connect width experts in their sofware of choice to learn. Similarly, the experts can charge a fee for their expertise and time. With this new users can get someone close or in the same geopraphical location where accounting practices can be similar or near similar to take away their onboarding pain.
